About Michael

Michael is happily working as a full-time software engineer. He enjoys devoting his time and attention to a variety of activities and intellectual pursuits. For example, in addition to hosting a popular radio talk show on KRLX, he has directed or produced numerous shows for the stage, including Agatha Christie's The Mousetrap, Sophie Treadwell's Machinal, and Eric Bogosian's Talk Radio.

He currently participates in TOOT-IN-COMMON a local, State College, flute choir and is an active member of the West Coast Swing scene. He enjoys cooking and baking and is an avid hiker.

Academically, he is currently taking graduate classes in Computer Science and Engineering at Penn State University. Previously, he majored in Computer Science and concentrated and carried out research in Cognitive Science at Carleton College. The results were presented at the Psyconomics conference in Fall of 2015.
