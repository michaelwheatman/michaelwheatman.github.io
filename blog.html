Amazon Affiliate

Today I signed up for the Amazon Affiliate program. I suppose that because I have a variety of interests and tend to both research and purchase stuff related to those interests I may as well post about them with links to the relevant items.

I've been very impressed with it. I had three specific requirements for a new laptop as a direct result of my previous one (a toshiba satellite).
